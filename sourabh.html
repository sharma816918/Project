Say No to Plastic

Plastic Facts

  • Over 8 million tons of plastic waste enter our oceans every year.
  • Plastic bags take around 100-200 years to decompose.
  • Microplastics have been found in over 80% of tap water samples around the world.

Effects of Plastic

  • Plastic pollution harms marine life and contaminates the food chain.
  • Plastic waste clogs our oceans and waterways, causing flooding and damage to ecosystems.
  • Microplastics have been linked to human health problems, including cancer and reproductive issues.
